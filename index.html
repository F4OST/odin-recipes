Our meal today features the classic Middle Eastern combination of falafel and ayran

Falafel is a delicious and nutritious vegetarian dish made from chickpeas, herbs, and spices,
and is often served in pita bread with a variety of sauces and vegetables
Ayran is a refreshing yogurt-based drink that helps cool down the spiciness of the falafel and complements its flavors perfectly
Together, these dishes make for a satisfying and flavorful meal that is both healthy and delicious

In addition to being a delicious meal
falafel and ayran have a rich history that reflects the diverse culinary traditions of the Middle East
Falafel is believed to have originated in Egypt or Palestine, where it was traditionally made with fava beans.
Over time, the recipe evolved to include chickpeas, which are now the most commonly used ingredient in falafel.
Today, falafel is enjoyed throughout the Middle East and beyond, and is a staple of vegetarian and vegan diets.
