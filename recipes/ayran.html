What Is Ayran:

Ayran is a traditional yogurt drink made by blending plain yogurt with cold water and salt.
It is popular in Central Asia, the Middle East, and the Mediterranean regions and is enjoyed as a refreshing beverage with meals.
Ayran is also a good source of probiotics, calcium, protein, and electrolytes.

Ingredients:

  • 2cups plain yogurt
  • 1/2cups cold water
  • 2 cloves, minced
  • 1/2teaspoon Salt to taste
  • Ice cubes for serving
  • Mint leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the yogurt and water until smooth and well combined.
  2. Add the minced garlic and salt to the bowl, and whisk again until everything is evenly mixed.
  3. Chill the Ayran in the refrigerator for at least 1hour before serving.
  4. When ready to serve, fill glasses with ice cubes and pour the Ayran over the ice.
  5. Garnish with mint leaves and serve immediately.
